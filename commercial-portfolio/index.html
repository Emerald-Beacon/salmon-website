Our Process

Salmon HVAC Commercial Proven Process

A clear, repeatable process from first call to final handover — so you always know where the project stands.

1
Step One

Initial Contact

Every project starts with a conversation. We take time to understand your building, your goals, and your constraints before anything else.

  • Client Inquiry — discuss project scope, system goals, and budget range
  • Project Brief — document load requirements, occupancy schedules, and building specs
  • On-site Inspection — walk the space to assess existing conditions, equipment, and access points
  • Compliance Check — confirm local code, permitting requirements, and ASHRAE standards
2
Step Two

Proposal

No surprises. You'll see exactly what you're getting, what it costs, and why — before anything is signed.

  • System Design — engineer a solution matched to your building's load, layout, and intended use
  • Cost Estimation — detailed line-item pricing with no hidden fees or vague allowances
  • Present Proposal — walk through the design together and answer questions before anything is finalized
  • Client Approval — we move forward only when you're confident and fully aligned
3
Step Three

Project Planning & Scheduling

Good installs start with good planning. We coordinate everything upfront so the project fits your schedule — not the other way around.

  • Create Timeline — milestone schedule built around your construction sequence and occupancy date
  • Coordinate Labor — align our crew, materials, and equipment deliveries with your GC and other trades
4
Step Four

Execution / Installation

Factory-trained technicians. Clean jobsite. Work done right the first time.

  • System Installation — equipment installed to manufacturer specs, applicable code, and your approved project drawings, with daily progress communication to your site team
5
Step Five

Testing / Handover / Documentation

We don't hand a system over until it's fully commissioned and your team knows how to run it.

  • Commissioning — full system startup, airflow balancing, and performance verification against design specs
  • Customer Training — walk your facilities staff through controls, filter schedules, and routine maintenance tasks
  • Documentation — as-built drawings, equipment schedules, warranty registration, and complete O&M manuals
  • Rebates — identify eligible utility rebates and assist with submission on your behalf
6
Step Six

Maintenance Plan & Post-Project Evaluation

The relationship doesn't end at handover. We stay engaged to protect your investment and make sure the system performs the way it was designed to.

  • Maintenance Plan — tailored PM schedule covering seasonal tune-ups, filter service, and priority dispatch to protect your warranty and avoid unplanned downtime
  • Post-Project Evaluation — review system performance at 30 and 90 days, close out any punch items, and confirm your team is fully satisfied
Commercial Capabilities

Full-spectrum commercial mechanical contracting.

From single-package rooftop units to building-wide VRF systems — we handle the equipment, the controls, the documentation, and the punch list.

Packaged Rooftop Units

2-ton through 50-ton RTU installation and service for retail, office, and light industrial. Carrier, Trane, and Daikin certified.

VRF / VRV Systems

Daikin VRV factory-authorized. Multi-zone variable refrigerant flow for medical offices, hospitality, and multi-tenant buildings.

Commercial Split Systems

Dedicated split system installs for medical suites, server rooms, and commercial kitchens with high-load conditioning needs.

Makeup Air & Ventilation

MAU design and install for commercial kitchens and industrial facilities. Code-compliant building pressurization.

Ductless Multi-Zone

Tenant-controlled ductless systems for multi-tenant offices, historic conversions, and restaurant dining rooms.

Commercial Heat Pumps

Cold-climate commercial heat pumps engineered for Utah winters. Capture utility rebates with high-efficiency installations.

Design-Build Coordination

Full HVAC design for new construction. We coordinate with architects, GCs, and other trades from schematic through commissioning.

Preventive Maintenance

Quarterly and semi-annual maintenance contracts with priority dispatch, filter inventory, and capital planning reports.

Emergency Service

2–4 hour dispatch on business days for commercial clients. Critical-facility priority protocols available by contract.
